==== Kensai Path Features ==== 
Permanent +2 AC bonus  <br />
Permanent additional attack  <br />
Kensai can only use melee weapons and throwing weapons.  <br />
A kensai cannot use magic except for Raise Dead, Resurrection, and Restoration, including from potions. Spell-like feats and innate abilities will function normally alongside spells cast from the spellbook of a Kensai class (Paladin / Ranger).  <br />
