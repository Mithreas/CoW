{{HeadingBlue|Welcome to Anemoi}}

[[Image:Anemoi.png]]

This is a Neverwinter Nights: Enhanced Edition persistent world with a focus on exploration and discovery, a land of strife and conflict where the things that everybody knows obscure hidden, deeper truths.  It is still very much a work in progress, but there's plenty of content to enjoy.

Have any questions? Want to join our little community? Join our [https://discord.gg/jn69WaU discord] and drop a line!
