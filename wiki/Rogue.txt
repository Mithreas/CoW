==== Notes, Tips, and Interesting Bits: ====   
Rogues get the most skill points of any class.   <br />
Rogues are the only class that can detect traps over DC 50 and disable traps over DC 35.   <br />
A rogues sneak attack damage will not stack with items providing sneak attack damage.   <br />
