==== Notes, Tips, and Interesting Bits: ====   
Create undead is only obtainable by having 3 blackguard levels.  It cannot be purchased later from the trainer.  <br />
The Blackguard's version of Bull's Strength will stack with other Bull's Strength spells.  <br />
Blackguard sneak attack will stack with any sneak attack or death attack granted by other classes or items.  <br />
